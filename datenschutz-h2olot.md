Privacy Policy for Water Tracker App
Last Updated: November 29, 2025
1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how the Water Tracker app (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using this App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect
The Water Tracker app is designed to be a fully local, privacy-focused water consumption tracking application. We collect the following types of information:

Water Intake Data: Records of when and how much water you drink (amount and timestamp)
App Settings: Your daily water goal, preferred unit of measurement (ml/oz), quick add values, and maximum daily total
Usage Statistics: Information about your usage patterns for calculating statistics like 7-day average, 30-day average, and streaks
Important Note: All data is stored exclusively on your device. The app does not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers or third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information
Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

To track your daily water consumption
To calculate and display your progress toward your water intake goals
To provide historical data and statistics
To enable features like the timer and export functionality
4. Data Storage and Security
All user data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and a local database
No data is transmitted to any external servers or cloud services
Data remains on your device unless you choose to export it (via the "Export Data" feature)
When you export data, the CSV file is shared through your device's native sharing functionality
The app does not have internet permissions and cannot send data without your explicit action
5. Data Sharing
The Water Tracker app does not share your personal data with any third parties.

The only time data leaves your device is when you explicitly choose to export it via the sharing functionality
When you export data, you control where it is sent (e.g., email, cloud storage)
The app does not contain any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
6. Data Retention
Your data remains on your device until you choose to clear it
You can clear all data at any time through the app's settings (Settings > Clear All Data)
If you uninstall the app, all data will be deleted from your device
7. User Rights
You have the following rights regarding your data:

Right to Access: View all your water intake data through the app's history
Right to Modify: Edit or delete any water intake entry
Right to Delete: Clear all data through the app's settings
Right to Export: Export your data as a CSV file for your personal records
8. Children's Privacy
The Water Tracker app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
